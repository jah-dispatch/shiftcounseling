About us

Shift Counseling Services is focused on providing substance abuse and mental health counseling services. We work with clients to achieve greater balance in their life. You will be challenged to Shift perspectives in your world to address mental health concerns, substance use, and other lifestyle imbalance.

Your happiness matters

Sometime people seek counseling because a family member encouraged them to do so. Others seek counseling because the courts ordered them to do so. Others are intuitive enough to know they needs some support and seek counseling for themselves. No matter how you got here you are now giving time and energy to the most important thing in your life, YOU. The decision to start counseling is the most difficult part. You have already begun the process, give us a call to help you make a positive Shift in your life.
