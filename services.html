General mental health counseling

Most people experience a time in their life when they are not feeling balanced. It may be a struggle with depression, anxiety, or adjustment to a new life challenge. Individual mental health counseling can help you cope with these challenges.

Evaluations

Substance abuse evaluations, mental health evaluations, anger management evaluations, and other evaluations are provided by Shift Counseling Services.

Evaluations at Shift Counseling are provided to determine the need and level of treatment required. Assessments produce a detailed report of the client's history, current situation, and recommendations for future needs.

Substance abuse counseling

Almost everyone's life is impacted by the use or abuse of someone close to us. No matter what substance is abused; alcohol, marijuana, methamphetamine, opiates, etc., there is a cycle of behavior that can be addressed to change the pattern of substance abuse. Counseling can support an individual struggling with substance use or a family member who is struggling with someone else's use.

Trauma – EMDR therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (now you know why it's called EMDR) is a treatment used to address trauma locked in the brain. This technique allows clients to release tension that has been building for weeks, months, or years.

Anger management

Anger is a natural emotion that we often struggle to manage. Anger management helps you understand your relationship with anger in order to manage it better. Remember, it is normal to get angry but it is important to learn how to express it appropriately.
